Welcome to the Woof Webpage!

This website is a great resource for potential dog owners looking for a one-stop shop for all information regarding dogs as pets, their history, different breeds, and more! If you are a new pet owner looking to learn more about the history of dogs, benefits of becoming a new dog owner, and next steps, refer to Dogs as Pets: A Short History! If you have made the decision that it is time to introduce a new furry friend into your family and are looking to decide what breeds will suit your lifestyle, refer to What Breed is For Me!
